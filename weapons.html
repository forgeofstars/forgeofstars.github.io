    Weapon Traits

    Agile

    The MAP you take with this weapon on an attack roll on your turn is -4 instead of -5 for each Attack power you’ve used so far this turn.

    Weapons
    Blaster holdout, Blaster pistol, Bolter pistol, Chain fist, Chain knife, Chain shortsword, Dart holdout, Dart pistol, Electrogel holdout, Electrogel pistol, Flame pistol, Ion pistol, Laser claws, Laser knife, Laser shortsword, Microsliver holdout, Microsliver pistol, Nanoedge claws, Nanoedge knife, Nanoedge shortsword, Rocket fist, Slug pistol, Stun fist, Unarmed strike

    Anti-shield

    Bonus damage on shields, less on people

    Weapons
    Emp grenade, Emp mine, Emp missile, Emp torpedo, Ion cannon, Ion pistol, Ion rifle, Ion turret

    AP

    This weapon is especially effective at piercing heavy armor. When you use this weapon to Strike a target with an AC (Armor) score, you gain a bonus to the attack roll equal to the weapon’s AP value or the enemy’s Armor value, whichever is smaller.

    Weapons
    Anti-materiel cannon, Anti-materiel rifle, Anti-materiel sniper, Anti-tank grenade, Anti-tank mine, Anti-tank missile, Anti-tank torpedo, Laser claws, Laser glaive, Laser knife, Laser scythe, Laser shortsword, Laser staff, Laser sword, Laser whip, Nuclear grenade, Nuclear mine, Nuclear missile, Nuclear torpedo, Railgun cannon, Railgun sniper, Thermal grenade, Thermal mine, Thermal missile

    Automatic

    Fires very rapidly

    Weapons
    Blaster machine gun, Blaster turret, Bolter machine gun, Bolter turret, Flame turret, Ion turret, Missile turret, Slug machine gun, Slug turret

    Backswing

    You can use the feedback from a missed attack with this weapon to lead into your next attack. After missing on a Strike with this weapon on your turn, you gain a +1 bonus to your next Strike with this weapon before the end of your turn.

    Weapons
    Blaster carbine, Bowcaster carbine, Electrogel carbine, Flame carbine, Rocket hammer, Slug carbine

    Blinding

    This weapon blinds creatures it hits. On a hit, the target is dazzled until the end of your next turn. On a critical hit, the target is instead blinded until the end of your next turn.

    Weapons
    Flashbang grenade

    Deadly

    On a critical hit, the weapon adds a damage die of the listed size. Add this after doubling the weapon’s damage. This increases to two dice if the weapon is Master quality and three dice if the weapon is Epic quality.

    Weapons
    Disruptor cannon, Disruptor rifle, Disruptor sniper, Laser scythe, Nanoedge rapier, Rocket scythe

    Disarming

    You can use this weapon to Disarm even if you don’t have a free hand. This uses the weapon’s reach (if different from your own). If you critically fail a check to Disarm using the weapon, you can drop the weapon to take the effects of a failure instead of a critical failure.

    Weapons
    Chain flail, Laser whip, Nanoedge rapier, Nanoedge whip, Rocket flail

    Downsized

    Counts as one size class smaller

    Weapons
    Blaster turret, Bolter turret, Flame turret, Ion turret, Missile turret, Slug turret

    Entangling

    This weapon entangles and traps creatures it hits. On a hit, the target is immobilized until the end of your next turn. On a critical hit, the target is instead restrained until the end of your next turn.

    Weapons
    Gravity grenade, Gravity mine

    Finesse

    You can use your Agility score instead of your Strength score on attack and damage rolls using this weapon.

    Weapons
    Chain knife, Chain shortsword, Laser claws, Laser glaive, Laser knife, Laser scythe, Laser shortsword, Laser staff, Laser sword, Laser whip, Nanoedge knife, Nanoedge rapier, Nanoedge shortsword, Nanoedge whip, Unarmed strike

    Forceful

    This weapon becomes more dangerous as you continue to attack with it. When you Strike with this weapon, you gain a bonus to the damage roll equal to the number of times you’ve attacked with it so far this turn.

    Weapons
    Bolter shotgun, Electrogel shotgun, Slug shotgun

    Free-hand

    This weapon doesn’t take up your hand, usually because it is built into your body or your armor. A free-hand weapon can’t be affected by Disarm. You can treat the hand covered by your free-hand weapon either as free or as wielding the weapon, whichever is more beneficial. For example, if you choose to treat the hand as free, you can wield other items, perform Manipulate actions, and so on, but you can’t attack with the free-hand weapon while doing so.

    Weapons
    Blaster wrist mount, Bolter wrist mount, Bowcaster wrist mount, Chain fist, Dart wrist mount, Missile wrist mount, Rocket fist, Slug wrist mount, Stun fist

    Messy

    Damage the environment on a crit miss

    Weapons
    Bolter cannon, Bolter machine gun, Bolter pistol, Bolter rifle, Bolter shotgun, Bolter turret, Bolter wrist mount, Chain axe, Chain fist, Chain flail, Chain glaive, Chain knife, Chain shortsword, Chain sword

    Parry

    This weapon can be used defensively to block attacks. While wielding this weapon, you can use a power to position your weapon defensively, gaining a +1 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn.

    Weapons
    Laser staff, Stun staff

    Poison

    On a critical hit, you deal the listed die as persistent poison damage.

    Weapons
    Chemical grenade, Chemical mine, Dart holdout, Dart pistol, Dart rifle, Dart sniper, Dart wrist mount, Nuclear grenade, Nuclear mine, Nuclear missile, Nuclear torpedo

    Reach

    This weapon is long, and your reach with it increases by 5 feet.

    Weapons
    Chain glaive, Laser glaive, Laser staff, Laser whip, Nanoedge glaive, Nanoedge whip, Rocket glaive, Stun glaive, Stun staff

    Reload

    After you fire this weapon, you must reload it before you can attack with it again. If the weapon has the reload 1 trait, then reloading it is a power. If the weapon has the reload 2 trait, then reloading it is a power.

    Weapons
    Anti-materiel cannon, Anti-materiel rifle, Anti-materiel sniper, Bowcaster carbine, Bowcaster rifle, Bowcaster sniper, Bowcaster wrist mount, Grenade launcher, Missile launcher, Torpedo launcher

    Shoving

    You can use this weapon to Shove even if you don’t have a free hand. This uses the weapon’s reach (if different from your own). If you critically fail a check to Shove using the weapon, you can drop the weapon to take the effects of a failure instead of a critical failure.

    Weapons
    Rocket axe, Rocket fist, Rocket flail, Rocket glaive, Rocket hammer, Rocket scythe, Rocket spear

    Silent

    This weapon makes no sound when attacking, making it idea for stealth missions or assassinations.

    Weapons
    Microsliver holdout, Microsliver pistol, Microsliver rifle, Microsliver sniper

    Small

    This weapon is very small and can be easily concealed under clothing. You have a +4 bonus to skill rolls made to hide this weapon.

    Weapons
    Blaster holdout, Chain knife, Dart holdout, Electrogel holdout, Laser claws, Laser knife, Microsliver holdout, Nanoedge claws, Nanoedge knife

    Smoke

    Explodes into obscuring fog

    Weapons
    Chemical grenade, Chemical mine, Smoke grenade

    Special Ammo

    Uses individual explosives as ammo

    Weapons
    Grenade launcher, Grenade throw, Missile cannon, Missile launcher, Missile turret, Missile wrist mount, Torpedo cannon, Torpedo launcher

    Stunner

    Can deal nonlethal Electric damage

    Weapons
    Blaster cannon, Blaster carbine, Blaster holdout, Blaster machine gun, Blaster pistol, Blaster rifle, Blaster sniper, Blaster turret, Blaster wrist mount

    Sweep

    This weapon makes wide sweeping or area attacks, making it easier to attack multiple enemies. When you attack with this weapon, you gain a +1 bonus to your attack roll if you already attempted to attack a different target this turn using this weapon.

    Weapons
    Blaster machine gun, Bolter machine gun, Chain axe, Chain flail, Flame carbine, Flame pistol, Flame rifle, Flame turret, Nanoedge axe, Rocket axe, Rocket flail, Slug machine gun

    Thrown

    You can throw this weapon as a ranged attack, and it counts as a ranged weapon when throws. A thrown weapon adds your Strength score to damage just like a melee weapon does. This trait lists the weapon’s range increment.

    Weapons
    Chain knife, Laser knife, Nanoedge knife, Rocket spear, Stun spear

    Tracking

    Can reroll the next round after a miss

    Weapons
    Torpedo cannon, Torpedo launcher

    Tripping

    You can use this weapon to Trip even if you don’t have a free hand. This uses the weapon’s reach (if different from your own). If you critically fail a check to Trip using the weapon, you can drop the weapon to take the effects of a failure instead of a critical failure.

    Weapons
    Chain flail, Laser scythe, Laser staff, Laser whip, Nanoedge whip, Rocket flail, Rocket scythe, Stun staff

    Unarmed

    An unarmed attack uses your body rather than a manufactured weapon. An unarmed attack counts as a weapon for game mechanics. Unarmed attacks are always free-hand.

    Weapons
    Unarmed strike

    Unwieldy

    Penalty for attacking nearby

    Weapons
    Anti-materiel sniper, Blaster sniper, Bowcaster sniper, Dart sniper, Disruptor sniper, Microsliver sniper, Railgun sniper, Slug sniper
