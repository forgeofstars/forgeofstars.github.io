    Thaumaturge

    Diviner You predict the future, see distant events, and track creatures with magic

    Fundamentalist You manipulate the fundamental laws of reality to control space, time, and magic
    Necromancer You channel necrotic energy to slay the living, then raise the dead to serve you

    Blaster You blast enemies away with devastating evocations of elemental energy

    Summoner You summon powerful magical creatures or spirits to serve you in combat
