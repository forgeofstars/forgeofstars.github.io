    Traits

    Acid

    Effects with this trait deal acid damage.

    Learned Powers
    Acid Arrow

    Attack

    A power with this trait is called an attack. An ability with this trait makes a special skill roll called an attack roll. For each attack you make beyond the first on your turn, you take a Multiple Attack Penalty.

    Basic Powers
    Disarm, Escape, Force Open, Grapple, Shove, Strike, Trip
    Learned Powers
    Acid Arrow, Disintegrate, Polar Ray, Powerbolt, Sleeper Hold

    Auditory

    Auditory effects rely on sound. An Auditory effect can only affect creatures that can hear it.

    Basic Powers
    Command Minion, Point Out, Talk
    Learned Powers
    Charming Words, Command, Dispiriting Taunt, Order, White Raven Tactics

    Cold

    Effects with this trait deal cold damage.

    Learned Powers
    Cone of Cold, Polar Ray

    Composition

    Powers with this trait involve a type of performance. Depending on what kind of performance you make, the power gains the Auditory or Visual traits. You can only have one Composition power active at a time; if you use a new Composition power, the previous one ends immediately.

    Learned Powers
    Dirge of Doom, Fatal Performance, Inspire Courage, Soothing Ballad

    Concentrate

    A power with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.

    Basic Powers
    Command Minion, Ready, Seek, Sustain, Take Aim
    Learned Powers
    Hunt Prey, Hunter’s Aim, Incredible Ricochet, Metamagic, Rage

    Daily

    Once you use a power with this trait, you cannot use that power again until you complete an 8-hour rest.

    Learned Powers
    Thrusters, White Raven Tactics

    Death

    An effect with this trait kills you immediately if it reduces you to 0 HP.

    Learned Powers
    Fatal Performance, Phantasmal Killer

    Detection

    Effects with this trait attempt to determine the presence or location of a creature or object.

    Learned Powers
    Mind Reading

    Electricity

    Effects with this trait deal electricity damage.

    Learned Powers
    Chain Lightning, Lightning Bolt

    Emotion

    This effect alters a creature’s emotions. Effects with this trait always have the Mental trait as well. Creatures that can’t feel emotions are immune to Emotion effects.

    Learned Powers
    Confusion, Dirge of Doom, Dispiriting Taunt, Fatal Performance, Fear, Inspire Courage, Phantasmal Killer, Rage, Soothing Ballad, Warp Mind

    Encounter

    Once you use a power with this trait, you cannot use that power again until you complete a 5-minute rest.

    Learned Powers
    Defensive Roll, Fatal Performance, Rage

    Fear

    This effect evokes the emotion of fear. Effects with this trait always have the Mental and Emotion traits as well.

    Learned Powers
    Dirge of Doom, Dispiriting Taunt, Fear, Phantasmal Killer

    Fire

    Effects with this trait deal fire damage or either conjure or manipulate fire.

    Learned Powers
    Fireball

    Flourish

    Powers with this trait require too much exertion to perform a large number in a row. You can use only 1 power with the Flourish trait per turn.

    Learned Powers
    Debilitating Shot, Double Shot, Flurry of Blows, Impossible Volley, Power Attack, Sudden Charge, Swipe, Whirlwind Strike

    Force

    Effects with this trait deal force damage or create objects made of pure magical force.

    Learned Powers
    Magic Missile, Resilient Sphere

    Fortune

    Effects with this trait beneficially alter how you roll your dice. You can never have more than one Fortune effect alter a single roll. If multiple Fortune effects would apply, you have to pick which to use. If a Fortune effect and a Misfortune effect would apply to the same roll, the two cancel each other out, and you roll normally.

    Learned Powers
    True Strike, Unity

    Healing

    An effect with this trait restores a creature’s body, typically by restoring HP, but sometimes by removing diseases or other debilitating effects.

    Learned Powers
    Lay on Hands, Soothing Ballad

    Incapacitation

    An ability with this trait can take a character completely out of the fight or even kill them. These abilities are harder to use on more powerful characters, such as player characters and powerful NPC bosses (as determined by the GM). These characters have a +10 bonus to saves, AC, or other defenses against Incapacitation effects.

    Learned Powers
    Charming Words, Color Spray, Dominate, Fabricated Truth, Master Strike, Sleeper Hold, Stunning Fist, Suggestion, Synaptic Pulse, Warp Mind

    Linguistic

    An effect with this trait depends on language comprehension. A Linguistic effect that targets a creature works only if the target understands the language you are using.

    Basic Powers
    Talk
    Learned Powers
    Charming Words, Command, Dispiriting Taunt, Order, Suggestion, White Raven Tactics

    Manipulate

    You must physically manipulate an item or make gestures to use a power with this trait. Creatures with a suitable appendage can’t use powers with this trait.

    Basic Powers
    Administer First Aid, Draw Item, Interact, Point Out, Release
    Learned Powers
    Metamagic

    Mental

    An effect with this trait can alter the target’s mind. It has no effect on an object or a mindless creature.

    Learned Powers
    Charming Words, Command, Confusion, Dirge of Doom, Dispiriting Taunt, Dominate, Fabricated Truth, Fatal Performance, Fear, Foresight, Inspire Courage, Mind Reading, Phantasmal Killer, Phantom Pain, Project Image, Rage, Soothing Ballad, Suggestion, Synaptic Pulse, Warp Mind

    Misfortune

    An effect with this trait detrimentally alters how you roll your dice. You can never have more than one Misfortune effect alter a single roll. If multiple Misfortune effects would apply, the GM decides which is worse and applies it. If a Fortune effect and Misfortune effect would apply to the same roll, the two cancel each other out, and you roll normally.

    Move

    A power with this trait involves moving from one space to another.

    Basic Powers
    Drop Prone, Leap, Stand, Step, Stride

    Open

    These maneuvers work only as the first salvo on your turn. You can use an Open power only if you haven’t used a power with the Attack or Open trait yet this turn.

    Learned Powers
    Deadly Aim, Impossible Volley, Point-Blank Shot, Sudden Charge, Whirlwind Strike

    Polymorph

    These effects transform the target into a new form. A target can’t be under the effect of more than one Polymorph effect at a time, later Polymorph effects disrupt existing ones. While you are polymorphed, your gear is absorbed into you; the passive abilities of your gear still function, but you can’t activate or use any items.

    Learned Powers
    Giant’s Stature

    Positive

    Effects with this trait heal living creatures with positive energy, deal radiant damage to undead, or manipulate positive energy.

    Learned Powers
    Lay on Hands

    Prediction

    Effects with this trait determine what is likely to happen in the near future. They are never guaranteed to give correct results, only guesses or possible outcomes.

    Learned Powers
    Augury, Foresight, Read Omens

    Press

    Powers with this trait allow you to follow up earlier attacks. A power with the Press trait can be used only if you are currently affected by MAP.

    Learned Powers
    Brutish Shove, Disarming Twist, Incredible Ricochet

    Revelation

    Effects with this trait see things as they truly are.

    Learned Powers
    True Seeing

    Scrying

    A scrying effect lets you see, hear, or otherwise get sensory information from a distance using a sensor or apparatus, rather than your own eyes and ears.

    Learned Powers
    Clairvoyance

    Secret

    The GM makes the skill roll for this ability in secret.

    Basic Powers
    Seek

    Shadow

    This magic involves shadows.

    Learned Powers
    Shadow Siphon

    Sonic

    An effect with this trait functions only if it makes sound, meaning it has no effect in an area of silence or in a vacuum. A Sonic effect might deal sonic damage.

    Learned Powers
    Sound Burst

    Spell

    See the Spell Rules page.

    Learned Powers
    Acid Arrow, Chain Lightning, Charming Words, Clairvoyance, Color Spray, Command, Cone of Cold, Confusion, Dimension Door, Disintegrate, Dispel Magic, Dominate, Fabricated Truth, Fear, Fireball, Foresight, Illusory Disguise, Illusory Object, Implosion, Invisibility, Lay on Hands, Lightning Bolt, Litany against Wrath, Magic Missile, Meteor Swarm, Mind Reading, Misty Step, Phantasmal Killer, Phantom Pain, Polar Ray, Powerbolt, Project Image, Read Omens, Redirect Gravity, Resilient Sphere, Shadow Siphon, Sound Burst, Suggestion, Synaptic Pulse, Time Stop, True Seeing, Warp Mind

    Stance

    A stance is a general combat strategy that you enter by using a power with the Stance trait, and that you remain in for some time. A stance lasts until you fall unconscious, until its requirements (if any) are violated, until the encounter ends, or until you enter a new stance, whichever comes first. You can be in a stance only during combat.

    Learned Powers
    Crane Stance, Cyberblade Stance, Disarming Stance, Dragon Stance, Dueling Dance, Mountain Stance, Multishot Stance, Point-Blank Shot

    Teleportation

    Effects with this trait allow you to instantaneously move from one point in space to another. Teleportation does not usually trigger reactions based on movement.

    Learned Powers
    Dimension Door, Misty Step

    Visual

    An effect with this trait can affect only creatures that can see it.

    Basic Powers
    Point Out
    Learned Powers
    Color Spray, Illusory Disguise, Illusory Object
